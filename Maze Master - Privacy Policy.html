Privacy Policy for Maze Master: Escape the Maze


Effective Date: October 17, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Hareb Al Bastaki ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you download and play our mobile game, Maze Master: Escape the Maze (the "Game").

By using our Game, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect a minimal amount of information necessary to operate the Game and provide its features.

A. Information Collected Automatically by Third-Party Services:

When you play our Game, our third-party partners may automatically collect certain information to provide core features and advertising. This includes:

  • Device Information: Your device model, operating system, and screen resolution.

  • Identifiers: Your device’s advertising identifier (IDFA for iOS, GAID for Android).

  • Gameplay Data: Information related to your progress, scores, and achievements for the purpose of leaderboards and game services.

  • General Location Data: Your IP address may be used to estimate your general location (e.g., country) to serve relevant ads.

B. Information You Provide to Us:

The only personal information we collect directly is what you voluntarily provide when you contact us for support, such as your email address and the content of your message.

C. Information We Do NOT Collect:

We do not collect sensitive personal information. All in-app purchases are securely processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, and we do not receive or store any of your payment or financial information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Operate the Game: To provide you with the gameplay experience and its features.

  • To Provide Online Features: To operate leaderboards and manage achievements through Google Play Games Services and Apple Game Center.

  • To Provide Advertising: To show relevant advertisements within the Game via Google AdMob, which helps keep the Game free.

  • To Validate Purchases: To restore your in-app purchases if you change devices.

  • To Provide Customer Support: To respond to your questions and support requests.

4. Sharing Your Information with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with the following trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the Game:

  • Google AdMob: Used to serve advertisements in the game. You can review their privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

  • Google Play Services: Used for in-app purchases and game features like leaderboards and achievements on Android devices. You can review their policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

  • Apple Game Center: Used for game features like leaderboards and achievements on iOS devices. You can review Apple's privacy policy here: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

5. Your Rights and Choices (Opt-Out of Interest-Based Ads)

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings.

  • On iOS: Go to "Settings" > "Privacy & Security" > "Tracking" and disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track" for all apps or specifically for this Game.

  • On Android: Go to "Settings" > "Google" > "Ads" and tap "Delete advertising ID".

Please note that you will still see ads, but they will not be tailored to your interests.

6. Children's Privacy

This Game is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age of consent in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at MazeMasterGame@gmail.com so we can promptly delete it.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the Game or on our website.
