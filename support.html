Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


Q: Is the game free to play?

A: Yes, Maze Master: Escape the Maze is free to download and play. The game is supported by ads and offers optional in-app purchases.

Q: Can I play the game offline?

A: Yes, the core gameplay is available offline. You can enjoy solving mazes anytime, anywhere, without an internet connection.

Q: How is my game progress saved?

A: Your gameplay progress, including completed levels and high scores, is saved locally on your mobile device. However, any in-app purchases you make are linked to your App Store or Google Play account and can be restored from the cloud.

Q: Do you have any tips for new players?

A: Absolutely! The key to success is observation. You have a few seconds to view the maze layout at the beginning of each level. Use that time to trace the correct path with your eyes before you start moving.


Reporting a Bug

  • Subject: Bug Report

  • Your Device Model: (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro, Samsung Galaxy S24)

  • Operating System Version: (e.g., iOS 18, Android 14)

  • A Detailed Description of the Problem: What happened? What were you doing in the game when it occurred?

  • Screenshots or Videos: If possible, a visual of the issue is extremely helpful!
